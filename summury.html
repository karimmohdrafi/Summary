Jersey

Arjun, a talented but failed cricketer, decides to return to cricket in his late thirties, driven by the desire to represent the Indian cricket team and fulfil his son's wish for a jersey as a gift.

This is an emotional film which portrays a person who is struggling to make it big in life at a later stage in his life. This film motivates us with a strong message that it's never too late to achieve as long as there's hardwork and dedication.


Pawan Kalyan

Pawan Kalyan is an Indian actor, politician, filmmaker, martial artist, and philanthropist who primarily works in Telugu cinema. Known for his unique acting style and mannerisms, he has a large fan base and a cult following.

Kalyan is a known philanthropist and is the founder of the Andhra Pradesh charityCommon Man Protection Force. He is a black belt in Karate and trains in various martial arts which he depicts in his films regularly. Kalyan is referred to as Power Star by his fans and in the media.
